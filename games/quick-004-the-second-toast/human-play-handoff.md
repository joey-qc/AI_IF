# Human Play Handoff: The Second Toast

## Player-Facing Setup

This is a classic Quick Mystery configured for medium difficulty. Images are off by default. All clues are available in text. Hints are available only on request.

## Opening Scene

Eleanor Vale's birthday dinner ended before the second toast could settle.

The dining room is still warm with candlelight and rain taps against the tall windows. Eleanor's chair sits slightly pushed back from the table. Beside her plate is a small amber cordial glass, untouched now except for the last sip she managed before whispering, "No. Not this."

Four people remain in the room: Julian Vale, her polished nephew and estate manager; Mara Vale, the niece Eleanor meant to bring back into the family foundation; Beatrice Hale, Eleanor's estranged daughter; and Felix Morrow, the solicitor who came with a document Eleanor never got to sign.

The question is simple: did Eleanor die naturally at a family dinner, or did someone make the second toast fatal?
