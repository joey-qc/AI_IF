# The Silent Gavel

## Player-Facing Setup

Judge Harrow's ceremonial gavel has vanished from a locked display niche during a brief courthouse reception. The chambers were busy but contained: a display niche, the judge's desk, a sideboard with tea service, shelves of case ledgers, and one door to the corridor.

The player is asked to identify who took the gavel and support the accusation with discovered evidence.

## Play Parameters

- Case ID: quick-002
- Length preset: quick_mystery

- Major NPCs: 3
