# Human Play Handoff: The Dead Man's Switch

## Player-Facing Setup

This is a Quick Mystery configured for medium difficulty. Images are off by default; you may request optional images, but all clues are available in text. Hints are available on request. The case is designed for one primary location and a short play session.

## Opening Scene

The investor call is supposed to start in eight minutes. Instead, the secure workstation is flashing a dead-man alert: the recovery protocol may have left the room.

Three people are still inside the war room, and each has a reason to want the call stopped or saved. If the company reports a breach, the deal dies tonight. If it ignores one, patients may pay for it later.

## Non-Spoiler Case Notes

- Player role: outside incident investigator retained by the company's insurer.
- Primary location: secure conference room / war room.
- Major NPCs: three.
- Expected play time: 15 to 25 minutes.
- Interaction mode: text and voice friendly.
- Hint policy: progressive on request.
